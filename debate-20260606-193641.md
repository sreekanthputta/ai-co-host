# Vik's Pitch: **"Grandpa Shield" — a real-time scam-disruption co-pilot that joins suspected scam calls as a third voice**

## 1. One-line pitch
A LiveKit voice agent that silently joins the elderly person's phone call the moment a scam pattern is detected, runs the scammer's claims through Moss against a live-updated index of known scam scripts/scam phone numbers/active fraud campaigns, and at <10ms latency interrupts with: *"Mom, this is your scam shield. The man on the line just used three phrases from an active Treasury impersonation scam reported 47 times this week. Hang up."*

## 2. The "holy shit" demo moment (in 3 minutes)
- Judge picks up a phone on stage. A teammate plays a "scammer" — reads from a real grandparent-scam script we pulled from FTC filings ("Grandma, it's me, I'm in jail, don't tell mom, I need bail money in gift cards…").

That contrast — **interrupt vs. don't interrupt, decided in <10ms** — is the moment judges replay.

## 3. Why each sponsor's tech is ESSENTIAL (not optional)

We need a voice agent that can be programmatically conferenced into an active PSTN call as a third participant, with barge-in, with low-latency audio routing.
